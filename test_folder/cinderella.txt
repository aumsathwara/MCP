Once upon a time, there was a beautiful girl named Cinderella. She lived with her stepmother and two stepsisters, who were very mean to her. One day, they were invited to a ball at the royal palace, but Cinderella was not allowed to go. However, with the help of her fairy godmother, she was able to attend the ball in a beautiful gown and glass slippers. The prince fell in love with her, but she had to leave before midnight, losing one of her slippers on the stairs. The prince searched for her and finally found her, and they lived happily ever after.
